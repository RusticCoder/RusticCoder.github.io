Rustic Coder

G'day, I'm the Rustic Coder here to offer up my opinions on technology, methodologies, and software strategies. With over three decades of technology under my belt all I can say is my Apple IIc was a beaut, but on to multi-core cloud computing.

Doing everything from writing, testing, and deploying the applications to creating and maintaining production servers. I've bounced around so much that not moving makes me queasy.
